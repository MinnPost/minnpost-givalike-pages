Please note: The card security code is not the last 3 or 4 digits of your credit card number.

About Donatic

Your donation is being processed by Donatic, a nonprofit organization specializing in donor advised funds. Donatic re-allocates your donation funds to your selected nonprofit(s). There are costs associated with processing and distributing online donations to your favorite nonprofit(s). A grant of 4.25% + $0.35 is allocated to Donatic from your total donation to cover all credit card processing fees, maintaining the technology to process and distribute your donations and to support Donatic’s mission.

100% of your donation is tax deductible.

As required by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS), Donatic has exclusive legal control over your contribution. The nonprofits you have selected were previously vetted by Donatic and Givalike; however, in the unlikely circumstances that there is a change that prevents Donatic from transferring your donation to your specified nonprofit, Donatic will work with you to specify a new nonprofit recipient.

If for any reason you are unsatisfied with your donation experience, Donatic will refund your contribution, less the sum that has already been distributed to your specified nonprofits.
